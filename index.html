History of Māori language (te reo Māori) in Aotearoa

A pepeha is a way of introducing yourself in te reo Maori. In Maori culture it is important to
identify yourself to your ancestors, where you are from and where you belong. Places such as
rivers, oceans, and mountains are important. The pepeha begins in the mountain, flows down
into the river, to the people and then to the sea or water
